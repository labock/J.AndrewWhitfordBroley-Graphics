EVALUATION—General Criteria

There are a total of 100 points possible in this course: 45 points for exams, 30 points for individual programming assignments and 25 points for the final programming project. The grade cutoffs will be A–94, AB–90, B–84, BC–80, C–74, CD–70 and D–60. All assignment and exam scores will be scaled to their point contribution in the final grade. I will only give an I/ grade to students who are unable to complete the course due to circumstances beyond their control.
