CSCI 321

EXAMS—Formal Learning Evaluation

There will be four in-class exams; each will scale to 15 points. The lowest of the four exam scores will be dropped, so that the total possible for exams will remain at 45 points. Exam dates are listed on the syllabus, and you are responsible for keeping those times clear. There will be no make ups for unexcused, missed exams. There is no comprehensive final exam.
